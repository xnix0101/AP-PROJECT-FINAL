The Heritage of Patek Philippe

Founded in 1839, Patek Philippe stands as the last independent, family-owned Genevan watch manufacturer. For over 180 years, the company has perpetuated the traditions of Geneva's fine watchmaking art, passing down exceptional expertise from generation to generation.

The company's grand complications are among the most sophisticated mechanisms in the world, with perpetual calendars, minute repeaters, and split-seconds chronographs representing the pinnacle of the watchmaker's art. Each timepiece is hand-finished to exacting standards that have become synonymous with the Patek Philippe name.

The Stern family, owners of Patek Philippe since 1932, uphold the company's commitment to excellence with their famous philosophy: "You never actually own a Patek Philippe. You merely look after it for the next generation." This sentiment reflects the enduring value and timeless quality of these extraordinary timepieces.
