The Heritage of Rado

Founded in 1917 in Lengnau, Switzerland, Rado has built its reputation as the Master of Materials, pioneering the use of high-tech ceramic in watchmaking. What began as a small atelier has evolved into one of the world's most innovative watch brands, known for its scratch-resistant and lightweight designs.

In 1962, Rado introduced the DiaStar, the world's first scratch-resistant watch, revolutionizing the industry with its innovative use of materials. This commitment to innovation continues today, with Rado consistently pushing the boundaries of what's possible in watchmaking technology and design.

Each Rado timepiece represents the perfect harmony between innovative material science and timeless Swiss craftsmanship, creating watches that are built to last for generations while maintaining their distinctive beauty.
